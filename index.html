What this site is

Engineering is often presented as either trivial or impossibly complex. In reality, most difficulty comes from missing context and skipped reasoning.

This site exists to break down real engineering problems the way they are actually solved — step by step, with assumptions made explicit and shortcuts called out.

How I approach problems

  • Start with intuition before equations
  • State assumptions clearly
  • Treat software results as hypotheses, not truth
  • Focus on why something works, not just how

The goal is not to memorise processes, but to build engineering judgement.

Where to start

  • The grid connection process
  • Power quality explained clearly
  • Scripting for repeatable studies

Each post walks through the full reasoning — not just the final answer.
